Our Process

How It Works

Our rigorous certification process ensures transparency, integrity, and consumer protection

1

Brand Application

Brands apply for Hamazon Health Certification through our comprehensive application process. Certification is renewed every 6 months to ensure ongoing compliance.

2

Product Selection

We carefully select 1 SKU per product line and send it to our rotating network of undisclosed third-party laboratories for comprehensive analysis.

3

Lab Analysis

Military-grade coordination protocols prevent manipulation and lab collusion. Our rotating lab system ensures unbiased, accurate results every time.

4

Public Results

All results are published online with complete transparency. Consumers get full access to ingredient analysis, safety assessments, and certification status.

Ingredient Database

Ingredient Blacklist

Problematic ingredients commonly found in kosher-certified products that harm your family's health

Red 40

High Risk

Artificial food coloring linked to hyperactivity in children, behavioral issues, and potential carcinogenic effects. Banned in several European countries.

Better Alternatives:

  • Beet juice powder
  • Paprika extract
  • Natural fruit extracts

MSG

Medium Risk

Flavor enhancer that can cause headaches, nausea, chest pain, and other adverse reactions in sensitive individuals. Often hidden under different names.

Better Alternatives:

  • Nutritional yeast
  • Sea salt
  • Natural herbs and spices

High Fructose Corn Syrup

High Risk

Processed sweetener linked to obesity, diabetes, metabolic disorders, and liver damage. More harmful than regular sugar due to processing methods.

Better Alternatives:

  • Pure maple syrup
  • Raw honey
  • Coconut sugar
