Our Process

How It Works

Our rigorous certification process ensures transparency, integrity, and consumer protection

1

Brand Application

Brands apply for Hamazon Health Certification through our comprehensive application process. Certification is renewed every 6 months to ensure ongoing compliance.

2

Product Selection

We carefully select 1 SKU per product line and send it to our rotating network of undisclosed third-party laboratories for comprehensive analysis.

3

Lab Analysis

Military-grade coordination protocols prevent manipulation and lab collusion. Our rotating lab system ensures unbiased, accurate results every time.

4

Public Results

All results are published online with complete transparency. Consumers get full access to ingredient analysis, safety assessments, and certification status.

Security & Integrity
